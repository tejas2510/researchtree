About ResearchTree 🌳

🚀 Our Mission

ResearchTree simplifies and amplifies collaboration between students and professors. Whether you're an innovator or a mentor, we help connect you to the right people and the right ideas, all from one centralized platform.

🎯 How It Benefits You

For Students

  • 🔍 Discover research opportunities across departments
  • 📄 Build a profile that showcases your interests & skills
  • 🎓 Gain research experience to boost your academics
  • 📊 Track your project involvement and contributions

For Professors

  • 🔎 Find the right students with specific skillsets
  • 📈 Manage and monitor project progress efficiently
  • 🤝 Collaborate with students and faculty seamlessly
  • 🏆 Showcase your work to the academic community

For the College

  • 📂 Monitor department-wide research initiatives
  • 📋 Track progress and publish outcome metrics
  • 🔗 Enable cross-disciplinary collaboration
  • 🏛️ Showcase institutional research strength

🛠️ Getting Started

Joining ResearchTree is simple:

  1. Login using your Google account
  2. Complete your profile with your research interests
  3. Explore or create research projects
  4. Collaborate, track progress, and innovate!
