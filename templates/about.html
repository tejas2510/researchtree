About the Research Collaboration Platform

Our Mission

The Research Collaboration Platform aims to streamline the collaboration between professors and students for research projects across various departments. By providing a centralized platform, we make it easier for students to discover research opportunities and for professors to find talented students to assist in their projects.

How It Benefits You

For Students

  • Discover research opportunities across different departments
  • Showcase your skills and interests to potential professors
  • Gain valuable research experience to enhance your academic profile
  • Track your involvement and contributions to various projects

For Professors

  • Find students with specific skills required for your research
  • Manage and track the progress of your research projects
  • Collaborate more effectively with students and colleagues
  • Showcase your research initiatives to the college community

For the College

  • Monitor research activities across different departments
  • Track progress and outcomes of research initiatives
  • Promote interdisciplinary collaboration
  • Showcase the institution's research capabilities

Getting Started

To get started with the Research Collaboration Platform:

  1. Login using your Microsoft account credentials
  2. Complete your profile with relevant information about your skills and interests
  3. Browse available research projects or create your own (for professors)
  4. Connect with professors or students to start collaborating
